Buko Pandan

Buko Pandan is a popular Filipino cold dessert made with jelly cubes, young coconut strips, and a creamy pandan-infused dressing. It is a refreshing staple at parties and gatherings, known for its distinct green color and tropical aroma.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bar (20g) Green agar-agar (gulaman)
  • 5 cups Water
  • 1 tsp Pandan extract (or fresh pandan leaves)
  • 2 cups Young coconut (buko), shredded
  • 1 can (370ml) Condensed milk
  • 1 can (300ml) All-purpose cream
  • 1/2 cup Small sago (tapioca pearls), cooked (optional)

Step by Step Guide

  1. In a pot, combine water, green agar-agar, and pandan extract. Stir until the agar-agar is dissolved.
  2. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Pour the mixture into a flat tray and let it cool until firm.
  3. Once the jelly is set, cut it into small cubes.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose cream and condensed milk. Whisk until the texture is smooth.
  5. Add the shredded young coconut, cooked sago, and the green jelly cubes into the cream mixture.
  6. Gently toss the ingredients until everything is evenly coated in the sweet cream.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the cream to thicken.
  8. Serve chilled in individual bowls or glasses.

Beef Kare-Kare

Beef Kare-Kare is a rich Filipino stew defined by its thick, savory peanut sauce and vibrant orange color from annatto (atsuete). It is traditionally served with a side of sauteed shrimp paste (bagoong) to balance the nutty sweetness.

Ingredients:

  • 500g tenderized beef
  • 1/2 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup Ground Toasted Rice
  • Annatto Water
  • Eggplant
  • Bok Choy
  • String Beans
  • Banana Heart
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Beef Broth
  • Fermented Shrimp Paste

Step by Step Guide

  1. Combine chicken pieces, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Remove chicken from the marinade (reserve the liquid) and brown the chicken pieces on all sides.
  3. Pour the reserved marinade and water into the pan. Add whole peppercorns and dried bay leaves. Bring to a boil.
  4. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Pour in the vinegar. Allow the liquid to re-boil, then simmer for another 10 minutes uncovered.
  6. Add sugar if you prefer a slightly sweeter taste. Stir and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot over steamed white rice.

Leche Flan

Leche Flan is the quintessential Filipino dessert—a rich, creamy steamed custard topped with a clear golden caramel slide. It is a staple at celebrations and "fiestas."

Ingredients:

  • Egg Yolks
  • Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2cup of Granulated Sugar
  • Water

Step by Step Guide

  1. Caramelize: Place sugar in the metal mold (llanera) over low heat until it melts into a golden brown syrup. Spread evenly and let cool.
  2. Mix: Gently whisk egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Do not create bubbles. Strain through a fine mesh or cheesecloth for a smooth texture.
  3. Pour: Pour the mixture into the llaneras and cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  4. Cook: Steam on medium-low heat for 30–35 minutes (or bake in a water bath/bain-marie at 180°C).
  5. Chill: Let it cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours before de-molding.

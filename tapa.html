Beef Tapa

Beef Tapa is a popular Filipino breakfast staple consisting of thinly sliced beef cured in a salty, sweet, and tangy marinade. It is the "Ta" in the famous Tapsilog (Tapa, Sinangag, and Itlog) combination.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Beef sirloin or round, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1 head Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 tbsp Cooking oil for frying

Step by Step Guide

  1. In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce, calamansi juice, minced garlic, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the beef slices to the marinade and toss until every piece is well-coated.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or ideally overnight, to let the flavors penetrate.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and add the cooking oil.
  5. Drain the beef from the marinade and place the slices in the pan in a single layer.
  6. Fry the beef for 3 to 5 minutes per side until the liquid has evaporated and the meat is browned and slightly charred.
  7. Serve hot with garlic fried rice (sinangag), a fried egg (itlog), and a side of spiced vinegar.
